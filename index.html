Welcome to Flow it!

Flow it can read text and generate into flowcharts. The resulting flowchart is not an image, but in the form of HTML and CSS that can adjust the shape of the screen. The results of the flowchart can be seen in the browser. More specifically if the screen is portrait then the flowchart flows down and if the landscape then the flowchart will flow to the side, thus minimizing scrolling webpage, enlarge and reduce the screen.

by Aldin, Fadhilah, Hasnan - Tekkom 2016
